# Governance

AlgoWhirl will transition toward decentralized governance through the DAO.

## Governance Token

AWT holders can participate in governance decisions.

Users who lock AWT receive veAWT which increases governance influence.

## Governance Scope

DAO governance may control:

• Protocol parameters
• Emission schedules
• Treasury allocations
• Future upgrades

## Long Term Vision

The governance model ensures the protocol evolves with its community rather than a centralized team.
